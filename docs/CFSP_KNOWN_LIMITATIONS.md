# CFSP Known Limitations

Use this list during private demos and design partner pilot conversations to keep expectations clear.

## Intentional Current Limits
- CFSP is not ready for public self-serve launch.
- Real email sending is not implemented.
- Microsoft Graph integration is not implemented.
- Microsoft Forms workflows are tracked and supported operationally, but CFSP does not create or sync Forms automatically.
- The CFSP Guide is rule-based and is not true AI.
- Billing, license management, and procurement workflows are not implemented.
- Institutional SSO and automated provisioning are not implemented.
- Calendar integrations are not implemented.
- Payroll, payment, and SP compensation workflows remain outside CFSP.

## Data Limits
- Do not store PHI in CFSP.
- Do not store student grades, assessment outcomes, remediation records, or student IDs in CFSP.
- Do not store real patient data.
- Demo data must be fake and clearly labeled.
- Pilot data should be limited to approved operational event, staffing, communication, training, material, and attendance information.

## SP Portal Limits
- SP portal users see only their own SP-facing information.
- SP portal users cannot see other SPs' responses, attendance, roster data, emails, phone numbers, private notes, invite history, raw invite URLs, token hashes, or admin-only notes.
- SP attendance is display-only for SP users in this phase.
- Portal invite links are sensitive and should be sent manually by an operator.
- Raw invite links appear only once when created.

## Pilot Operating Limits
- CFSP should run alongside existing workflows during early pilots.
- Operators should manually confirm assignments and communications before relying on CFSP as the source of record.
- Any spreadsheet import or legacy data cleanup should be reviewed by a human.
- Design partner pilots should be scoped to a small number of events and users before any annual license conversation.
