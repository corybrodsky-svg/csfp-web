# CFSP Pilot One-Pager

## Positioning
CFSP is a simulation operations command center for healthcare simulation programs. It gives simulation teams one place to plan events, coordinate SP staffing, manage communication workflows, track readiness, and run day-of operations.

CFSP is built for private design partner pilots, not public launch. The goal is to validate the operating workflow with real simulation teams while keeping scope, privacy, and expectations clear.

## Who It Is For
- Simulation center directors
- Standardized patient program directors
- Clinical skills leaders
- Nursing, PA, medical education, and interprofessional simulation administrators
- Sim Ops teams responsible for staffing, training, materials, and day-of coordination

## Problems CFSP Helps Address
- SP staffing lives across spreadsheets, inboxes, calendar notes, and memory.
- Availability responses are hard to compare against event dates, times, and room needs.
- Confirmation emails and follow-ups are easy to miss.
- Training readiness, materials, Zoom links, recordings, and case notes drift across tools.
- Day-of check-in and room coverage can be unclear until a problem is already visible.
- SP portal adoption is uneven; some SPs can use a portal while others still need email, Microsoft Forms, phone, or manual handling.

## What CFSP Does In The Current Pilot-Ready Loop
- Creates and manages simulation events in an operations command center.
- Tracks SP coverage, assignments, responses, and attendance.
- Supports lightweight SP shift offers and portal responses.
- Shows SP communication coverage across portal, email, Microsoft Forms, manual, and do-not-contact modes.
- Supports portal invite onboarding without sending real email automatically.
- Gives SP users a privacy-safe portal for their own open shifts, responses, upcoming items, and attendance status.
- Keeps first-time users oriented with a rule-based CFSP Guide.
- Provides demo-safe seed data, a demo operator page, smoke tests, and pilot-readiness docs for private walkthroughs.

## What CFSP Is Not Yet Claiming
- Not a replacement for institutional identity management, Microsoft Graph, SMTP, LMS, EMR, or scheduling systems.
- Not a public self-serve SaaS launch.
- Not a true AI assistant.
- Not a tool for PHI, student grades, or real patient data.
- Not a fully automated communications engine; pilot communication workflows still require human review and manual sending.

## Pilot Conversation Ask
Would a 30-60 day design partner pilot help your team test whether a simulation operations command center reduces coordinator friction around staffing, communication, readiness, and live event coordination?
